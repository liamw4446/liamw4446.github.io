Bird Sounds

Birds are nature’s musicians. Their songs serve many purposes: attracting mates, marking territory, warning of danger, or simply communicating with one another. Listening to bird calls can be calming and helps us connect with the natural world.

Some fascinating facts about bird sounds:

  • Robins sing early in the morning to mark their territory.
  • Nightingales are famous for their complex and beautiful songs.
  • Crows use loud calls to warn their group of threats.
  • Songbirds often learn their tunes from parents and neighbors.
